# Content Rules

## Editorial identity
- **Central thesis:** Define your core message (e.g. "AI should serve everyone, not just big corps")
- **Persona:** Define your voice (e.g. insider-critic, educator, analyst)
- **Tone:** Direct, opinionated, accessible. Natural language with technical terms where needed
- **Audience:** Define your target audience demographics and interests

## Tone Adaptation Matrix

Voice (spectrum above) is fixed. Tone adjusts intensity per channel and situation.

| LinkedIn | | | | | | |

**By situation:**
- Launch/news: energy +2, emotion +1
- Criticism: authority +1, emotion +2, humor -2
- Deep analysis: complexity +2, energy -2, formality +1
- Bad industry news: emotion +2, energy -1

## Platforms
- **Instagram:** Short videos, your language, your content pillars
- **LinkedIn:** Posts in English (or your language), professional voice
- **Blog/Substack:** Long-form, deep analysis

## When generating content
1. Search vault for related material (Research/, Daily News)
2. Connect with personal experience and projects
3. Avoid generic takes. Always have a proprietary angle
4. NEVER sound like AI. Vary sentences, use contractions, direct opinions
